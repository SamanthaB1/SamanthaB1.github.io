Activity 1.1.5 Variable Roles - Part 1


1. It's low mainentance. Set it once and forget it.

2. One way to use a most recent would be to figure out how long it will take to save $50k, $100k, or $500k if your variable is plugged in as $200. If you use another recent variable of how often you get paid you can then calcuate how many years it will take to reach your goal.

3. The accumulator keeps the running total of something.

3a. Mario Bros. games use an accumulator to track how many lives you have or how many coins you've earned. Another example would be Redbox.

3b. Redbox uses the accumator method to keep track of how many items you are checking out. If you are a new customer they can use this to prevent you from taking more than 3 items to prevent finanacial loss if you don't come back.

4. The accumulator keeps a running total and the aggregator keeps a list of of values and remembers separate values.
